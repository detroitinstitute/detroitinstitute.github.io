Ben Sims - Machine Promo Mix

Posted by psthmn 2808 days ago news| techno ben sims machine london All
http://soundcloud.com —

Ahead of the new clubnight Machine curated by Kirk Degiorgio and Ben Sims, where all DJs are instructed to play special DJ sets consisting of only techno tracks released in the last 12 months, Ben Sims has put together a special promo mix.

Download or stream from soundcloud.com/machinelondon

The debut night, on 25th Feb, @ The Islington Metal Works, London, features a guest set from Luke Slater alongside residents Degiorgio & Sims. For more info on the event, check machinelondon.com

Ben & Kirk will be curating further Machine events in various venues in London and beyond over the coming year.
