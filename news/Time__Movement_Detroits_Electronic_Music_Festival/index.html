Time for Movement: Detroit's Electronic Music Festival

Posted by tomo 4163 days ago news| detroit demf festival event All
http://www.demf.com — Finally, it's Memorial Day weekend again and that means it's time for the Detroit Electronic Music Festival! Or Movement, Fuse-In, TechFest, Tec Fest, whatever you want to refer to it as, we just pronounce it DEMMMMF. Anyways, I'll be occupied with it all weekend and I feel sorry if you too can't make it. Or to its plethora of afterparties, listed on Detroit Luv.
