[RTPOD-6]: DJ Parket - Last Rotation

Posted by vadz 3356 days ago techno| download podcast techno offbeat idustrial russia set mix All
http://www.russian-techno.com —

DJ Parket from Rostov's promo-group Illegal Project (which is known for interesting underground events in abandoned industrial places), presents his excursion through the world of offbeat techno with industrial elements in our next Russian Techno podcast episode. His selection includes big names as well as artists hosted on independent netlabels like Ante Rasa. Overall, the mix is dark and strong, scary but warm! Enjoy the trip and feel the atmosphere of a proper south-russian illegal rave!
