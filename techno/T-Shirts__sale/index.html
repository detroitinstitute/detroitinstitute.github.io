T-Shirts... for sale?

Posted by tomo 4159 days ago techno| demf tshirt All
DEMF is over and a good time was had by all. Some of you probably saw me walking around with a Detroit Institute of Techno t-shirt and I got interest from several in getting a shirt of their own. If there is enough interest I will print up few and send them to those who request them at the price I'm considering of $10. So if you are interested please tell me if you'd be okay paying $10 + shipping and what size by sending an email to tomo@detroitinstitute.org or tshirt@detroitinstitute.org.
