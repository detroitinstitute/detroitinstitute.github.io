Go Hiyama and Takaaki Itoh at Groove

Posted by tomo 4198 days ago techno| japan download All
If you enjoyed the set by Takaaki Itoh last week you'll probably dig the performance by him and his Japanese music partner Go Hiyama recorded at Groove in Madrid where Go has a large following. This week's Takaaki set is two hours long and not as hard as last week's, closer to Detroit, but it certainly has its moments. It does seem to have some problems skipping, however. IMO, the Go set is the better of the two with some great harder tracks.
