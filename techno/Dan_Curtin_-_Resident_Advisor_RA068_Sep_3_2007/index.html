Dan Curtin - Resident Advisor RA.068 (Sep 3 2007)

Posted by tomo 4060 days ago techno| download funky All
Dan Curtin, who honed his skills in near isolation in the Cleveland, Ohio area, first found support on Detroit label 33 RPM before catching real notoriety on Peacefrog and his own label, Metamorphic, a label that has seen releases from a motley crew of producers such Morgan Geist, Detroit's Terrence Parker, and Titonton, also from Ohio.

Dan is up this week on Resident Advisor's podcast.
