Jacbri Radio Show 01.05

Posted by tomo 4174 days ago techno| download radio croatia hard industrial All
Jacbri Radio Show 01.05. w/ special guest mix by Mimi Live: Kvarnerski Radio. This show has two parts with the second part mixed by Croatian DJ Mimi from Zagreb. It's a nice diverse mix with lots of hard distorted techno and sprinkling of industrial and other hard underground sounds, even dubstep.
