Detroit Institute of Techno - Dubtronica Experiments http://www.detroitinstitute.org/techno/dubtronica-experiments-1/ Dubtronica consists of electronic beats played in a downbeat manner. It is much lower in speed than techno and generally warmer than dance-oriented electronica. The genre is repetitive but rewards careful listener attention.
