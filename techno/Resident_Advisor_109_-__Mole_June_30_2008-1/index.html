Resident Advisor 109 - The Mole, June 30, 2008

Posted by tomo 3739 days ago techno| download detroit funk r&b disco soul All
Resident Advisor podcast issue 109 from The Mole isn't exactly techno but it's definitely got Detroit. Enjoy something a little bit different.
"You gotta feel for Colin de la Plante. His Modern Deep Left Quartet pals went off and formed Cobblestone Jazz and now tour the world without him. His girlfriend broke up with him. And just this past week he broke his collarbone*, which explains why we didn't immediately have a tracklist ready for you. Can you blame him for making a mix entitled Soft Hits for Making Up and Breaking Up?"
