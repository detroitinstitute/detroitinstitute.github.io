April 2007 Edition of the Cynet-Media Podcast

Posted by tomo 4188 days ago techno| download detroit japan podcast disco soul All
The fifth podcast from Claude Young's Cynet-Media features two sets from DJs GA9 & Kuniharu Yoshida.

DJ GA9 is one of Cynet-Media's team members. His mix features a host of tasty contemporary dance cuts. Lots of smooth vocal tracks, some west London type stuff.

Kuniharu Yoshida is the legendary father of dance music from Fukui Japan.He was the first DJ in Fukui and this set is ripe with classic disco and soul music.
