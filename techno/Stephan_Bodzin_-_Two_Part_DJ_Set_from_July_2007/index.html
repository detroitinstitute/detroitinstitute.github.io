Stephan Bodzin - Two Part DJ Set from July 2007

Posted by tomo 4073 days ago techno| download electro minimal bremen All
Stephan Bodzin - Two Part DJ Set from July 2007. The crazy heads haved electro minimalist German producer has had requests to do remixes for the likes of Depeche Mode ("Everything Counts" with Oliver Huntemann), Yello ("Oh Yeah".. did this make Richie Hawtin jealous or did this come too long after the release of "Orange"), Booka Shade and The Knife and releases on Cocoon, Systematic, Get Physical, Datapunk, Gigolo, Systematic and Giant Wheel not to mention his own label Herzblut.

Download mp3 of part 2

And if you want more check out Stephan Bodzin - Session Deluxe mix from May and also some video from his performance at Studio 88 in Aix-en-Provence, France that was mentioned there. Unfortunately, Studio 88 no longer has the actual mix available. If you'll be in that region in September check out Kero's show on September 14th.
