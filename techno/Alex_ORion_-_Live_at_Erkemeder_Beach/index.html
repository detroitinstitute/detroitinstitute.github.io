Alex O'Rion - Live at Erkemeder Beach

Posted by tomo 4096 days ago techno| download deep house holland All
Dutch DJ and producer Alex O'Rion returns with a new 2.5 hour mix taken from a 6 hour set recorded live at Erkemeder Beach. Says Alex, "It begins with melodic progressive deephouse and it builds up to some harder, but still melodic stuff." If you enjoyed Alex's previous oldschool techno and deep techno sets and I know many of you did then you may want to check this one out.
