Ellen Allien - Live on JJJ's The Club 09/06/2007

Posted by tomo 4138 days ago techno| download radio berlin detroit australia All
Ellen Allien - Live on JJJ's The Club 09/06/2007. Berliner and founder of BPitch Control on the Australian radio program The Club on Triple J. Starts off with Carl Craig's hot remix of "Like a Child" which is described by Resident Advisor this way:

"Craig strips it all back to great effect, speeding things up for the dance floor and leaving only a continuous loop of the opening melody. While the original is a slow dreamy melodic number, Craig creates a building intensity, really stretching it out. After working you up for a good four minutes, the beat finally drops and you are left with a sense of satisfaction that Carl has come through again; all thatâ€™s left is to shake your ass and ride it on home."
