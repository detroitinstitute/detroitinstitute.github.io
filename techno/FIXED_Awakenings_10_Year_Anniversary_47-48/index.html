FIXED: Awakenings 10 Year Anniversary 4/7-4/8

Posted by tomo 4201 days ago techno| download streaming detroit tech house minimal All
This is the rest of the Awakenings 10 Year sets that I've found. The first night's sets are here except for Surgeon's set here. In order of appearance:

Kenny Larkin, second wave Detroit techno artist who first released on Plus 8 Records. There are two sets floating around the net purporting to be the Awakenings 10 Year set but the one of them is fake. Be sure you get the right one, the first download link, an appropriately Detroit set.

Download rar of another Kenny Larkin set from rapidshare

Dave Clarke, who we've mentioned before.

Stream
Download rar from rapidshare
Download zip from another annoying upload site (never got this to work)

Billy Nasty, a UK dj who comes from a progressive house background who has toured the globe many times; a lesser-known dj in North America but he has played in New York City, Chicago, Detroit, Washington, San Francisco, LA, Toronto, and Seattle. It's a bit on the ravey side and the sound quality doesn't help.

Remy, dutch dj who likes "to play a variety of sounds from tech-house, to techno, to English progressive, to trance". He closed the second night of the event.

Download mp3 from megaupload

Wighnomy Brothers with a light, techy, eclectic set to help begin the last night of the fest.

Marco Carola's and Green Velvet's sets have not yet been released, unfortunately. Catch Marco Carola adds DEMF next month.

Oscar Mulero from Spain. Note: the set is this labeled as being Kenny Larkin.

Download mp3 from rapidshare

Gayle San from Singapore closed the festivities. She has had residencies at some of the top techno clubs and played at some of the most underground clubs in the world, like Bionic in Detroit, Pure Pleasure parties in Chicago, Soma in Montreal, Twilo in New York, Zouk Club in Singapore, Florida 135 in Spain, Fuse Club in Belgium. A good live DJ set of techno.
