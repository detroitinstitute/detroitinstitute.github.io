Dirty Work - Dave McNasty (Dirty Electro - Summer 07)

Posted by tomo 4066 days ago techno| download dirty electro unreleased All
DIRTY WORK - Dave McNasty (Dirty Electro - Summer 07!). Dirty filthy electro bass from McNasty. Riddled with new and unreleased tracks and remixes.

Dave McNasty: "I just may have outdone myself again with the amount of huge, filthy bass in this mix. It's got a bunch of new/unreleased tracks, along with a few remixes of classics. If you're a fan of electro and bass this is sure to please - A filthy mix of some of the hottest tracks from Summer 2007. Don't snooze."
