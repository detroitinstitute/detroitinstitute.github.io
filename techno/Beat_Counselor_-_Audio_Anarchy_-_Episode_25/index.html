Beat Counselor - Audio Anarchy - Episode #25

Posted by tomo 4117 days ago techno| download electro house funky dirty acid All
Beat Counselor - Audio Anarchy - Episode #25. "ElectroHouse. RobotRock. FunkyTechno." Full of dirty electro basslines.

"Ten years after Daft Punk first dropped "Around the World," their influence can still be heard in the new wave of French dance music. It's that future-retro sound. It's that fusion of synth-pop, techno, house, acid-house and electro. It's robot rock."
