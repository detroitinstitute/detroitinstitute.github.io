Patrick Bodmer and Philipp Jung have for years been making music that has what it takes to top European charts while still appealing to techno sensibilities. From time to time they, as M.A.N.D.Y., team up with fellow Deutschlanders Booka Shade who share this similar style which leans towards disco-pop electro-house minimal-techno.

And so it goes on their set broadcast on Dasding Plattenleger, a weekly weekend radio show hosted by Stephan Hinz broadcast on FM in South Germany and over the internet.
