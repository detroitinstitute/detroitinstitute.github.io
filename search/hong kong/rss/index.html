Akira Ishihara (a.k.a. A-inc), minimal techno producer and DJ from Tokyo, Japan

"At age of 18, He started DJ and sound making. Then, he sent the demo CD to some labels, and released 12 inches from "kiddaz.fm", "kazumi" and "SUBmissions" in Spring-Summer. After that, he released many club tracks which have many voice sample & pumpi'n beat. Each has acquired popularity from many DJ.

He went Germany in 2003 winter and 2004 Summer for DJ tour.DJ at some places containing "Electric Ballroom" at SO36. He played not only Japan, but also Gemany, Austria, Hong-Kong, Taiwan and China."
