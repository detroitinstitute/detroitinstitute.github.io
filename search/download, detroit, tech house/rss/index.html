Adam Jay & Brian Kage is a heavy load of experience, know-how, innovative sound design
and more in the centre of technoid elctronics.Their Bios are full of headwords as Azure
Records, Primevil, DLuv Recording, Lumina, Primate, Alphabet City, Intec, Cocoon, V2 Recording

A1.: Unfuckwithable" is a timeless, beautiful and incredibly wide track! The beat sets up
extreme pressure - deeply pumping! The introducing bass creates an nameless groove with
great harmonics. A tiny squeaky synth behaves like a ball lightning and adds a particular
charm to the track. The producers are rolling out such an extraordinary carpet of sound that
the eight-minute arrangement is over too quick and you want to enjoy again and again. Great
track - full of emotion and warm atmosphere - far in the front and at home at any dance floor -
every time and everywhere!

A2: Simplicity Always Wins" You are going completely wrong if you would like to judge
the track from its name. You could think that the name is suitable in the beginning - hearing
the airy low-groove beat. Next step you get in touch with the constant bass and the forcing up
synths and effects and realize that the guys attached importance to extreme sonic quality.
A few elements merge and create a complex environment which you will listen in repeat mode until
you experienced every single bit. It's a ticket for a rich in content vacation at the Monoid universe!

B1.: Meridian B-Side surprise - extraordinary dance floor bomb. Contrasts attract each
other - celebration par excellence! The beat pushes and agitates the feet on the dance floor.
The harmonic bass supported by the creamy and clear chords and ambience bursts the firm
atmosphere and you can hardly describe the created mood. This track provides a state of
emergency! Headbanger with strong emotions - must listen!
