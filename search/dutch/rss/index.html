The 102nd Podcast of my weekly Podcast show Invite's Choice!
This week with guest: Giorgio Gigli

Starting today, I will be uploading an extra podcast every last Saturday of the month.
Besides this mix by Giorgio Gigli, Raffaella Attanasio's electronic sounding podcast will be also be online on my SC page!

It's been a while since I first asked Giorgio Gigli for a podcast, but the moment is finally here. He's made a 61 minute trip that stays interesting from the first to last minute and doesn't bore at any time.
Often really deep sets have that effect on me as they're to flat, but not so for mr Gigli: he knows what he's doing!

What else can I say about this man who has released records on Electric Deluxe, label of Dutch hero Speedy J and where I was fortunate enough to play at Timewarp Holland, Giorgio has played at the Italian edition!

It's about time to get to the music; here's Giorgio Gigli's trip for Invite's Choice!
