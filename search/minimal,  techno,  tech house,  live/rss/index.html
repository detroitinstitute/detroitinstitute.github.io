Atomic Jam returns to the Q Club for one of only two planned spectacular shows at this venue in 2010. Luke Slater, one of UK's true electronic music & techno pioneers, presents his Planetary Assault Systems live show. As well as running his own label Mote-evolver, 2009 saw the release of the new and much awaited PAS album on Ostgut Ton in Berlin, again changing the Techno landscape to much praise from fans and industry alike. Frequency 7, a soundclash of styles from Ben Sims and Surgeon, performing together creating a uniquely improvised experience combining elements of techno, funk, dub, house, hip-hop, disco, industrial etc. utilizing turntables, CD decks, FX & all the latest Hardware/Software technology. Redshape's sound sits somewhere between everything in dance music and Redshape's very own imagination. It's not minimal, not maximal and always historically respecting the vibe of Detroit. Repetition is something Redshape works hard to prevent, his live shows morph & develop everytime to never play the same show twice. Residential duties fall to the much talented Mark Broom & Chris Finke, who both have a wealth of releases, remixes and collabs to keep your eyes on this year

recorded live at my own monthly, down was created out of my own personal desire to have something different to attend, as well as giving other music connoisseurs a new option in things to hear on a thursday night. more of a listening party than a dance party, the monthly has been growing in strength since its inception five months ago, having already featured guests such as insideout and derek plaslaiko, with future guest sets from adultnapper, dj three, and many more coming.

having the option to play music that they love to purchase but never get to play, down has been on dj's lists of events to perform at since they get to stretch their musical legs and try something different.


this set, recorded live in the mix on 11.20.08, comes in at almost five hours in length. starting at around 116 beats per minute and ending around 120, its a slow, sexy journey through my heart and soul.


enjoy...
