Home with House ep21 - Deep Avenue

9:44 PM August 16, 2009
Hey everyone, it's time for another Home with House podcast episode! Episode 21 is an hour long mix of deep and progressive house. It starts off relatively low key and mellow with some moody deep house containing a few hints of progressive house, and it gradually builds in momentum to end with a couple of big progressive tracks.

This mix has an uplifting and ethereal vibe to it. Many of the tracks feature simple yet pleasing piano chords and break downs which generally add to that nice ethereal feel of the mix. Something about haunting piano music, even when used minimally in house music, really appeals to me and I think it fits well with this deep vibe. This episode is called "Deep Avenue".

Posted by:  MadTee  on August 19, 2009 8:33 AM

Thanks for the feedback everyone.

As for the low quality (128 kbps) podcast, this is something I considered a long while ago. In fact, I record all my mixes in WAV format for optimal quality, then convert them to a lower mp3 format for sharing on the web. You'll notice my other non-podcast mixes are offered in 320kbps on this website as it is MUCH better quality. But the file size is much larger. The podcast is something that is typically streamed alot over the web and as such, it needs to be fast. Podcasts are typically 128kbps because of this. I thought about offering both a 128kbps and 320kbps version of the podcast, but then everything to do with podcast tracking (and programs like itunes, etc) would get confused about what version to use and how many episodes there are when there are 2 versions of each episode. So I decided to stick with the 128kbps quality for the podcast to keep things relatively simple.

However, if you would like a higher quality version of a specific one of my mixes, let me know and I'll put a 320kbps version on my site for you to grab.
